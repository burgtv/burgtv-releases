How to install

Simple step-by-step guides for each platform.

1

Enable app installation from unknown sources

On your Fire TV, go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer options and enable "Apps from Unknown Sources". On Android TV go to Settings → Device Preferences → Security → Unknown sources.

2

Install the Downloader app

On your Fire TV, search for "Downloader" app (by AFTVnews) on Amazon Appstore and install it. It's free. On Android TV boxes you may already have a file browser that supports URL download.

3

Open Downloader and type this URL

Open the Downloader app. In the URL bar, type:

download.burgtv.com/tv.apk
4

Download and install

Click Go. The APK will download (about 17 MB). When it's done, click Install. Accept permissions.

5

Launch BurgTV and configure your source

Open BurgTV from your Fire TV home screen. Add your Xtream Codes credentials or M3U playlist URL when prompted. Enjoy!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before installing BurgTV.

Is BurgTV legal?
Yes. BurgTV is a neutral media player, similar to VLC, MX Player, or Kodi. It does not host, distribute, or provide any content. You are responsible for ensuring that the content you access through your own media source is legal in your jurisdiction. See our Terms of Service.
Does BurgTV provide channels or streams?
No. BurgTV does not provide any content. You must supply your own Xtream Codes credentials, M3U playlist URL, or connect to your self-hosted media server (Plex, Jellyfin, Emby).
Why is BurgTV not on Google Play / App Store / Amazon?
Many official app stores have strict policies about IPTV-capable players, even when the app is legal and neutral. We distribute directly via download.burgtv.com to avoid the back-and-forth with store reviews. We are also available on APKPure, Huawei AppGallery, and other alternative stores.
Is it safe to install an APK outside the Play Store?
Yes, when you trust the source. BurgTV APKs are signed with our official release keystore and the SHA256 hash is published with each release on GitHub Releases. You can verify the APK integrity before installing. Android may warn you (Play Protect) because the app is not from Play Store — this is expected and not a sign of malware.
Does BurgTV work with my IPTV subscription?
BurgTV supports any IPTV subscription that provides Xtream Codes credentials (username + password + portal URL) or an M3U/M3U8 playlist URL. It does not include or endorse any specific IPTV provider.
Does BurgTV collect my data?
No ads, no tracking, no analytics. The only data collected are anonymous crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics (only in release builds) to help us fix bugs. See our Privacy Policy. GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, nFADP compliant.
Is there a Premium version?
BurgTV is free. An optional Premium subscription is available via app.burgtv.com for users who want cloud favorites, multi-device sync, and priority support. 7-day free trial. Premium is NOT required to use the app.
How do I update BurgTV?
BurgTV has a built-in update checker. When a new version is available, you'll see a notification. You can also manually check by re-downloading from this page or visiting GitHub Releases.
How can I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us at info@burgtv.com. We typically respond within 24–48 hours.
How do I request DMCA takedown?
If you believe the app is being misused to access copyrighted content, contact us at info@burgtv.com with the required information. See our DMCA Policy.
